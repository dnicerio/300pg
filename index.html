300 Perfect Game

A gag manga series created by Jeff Nice

Plot Summary

The web manga follows the everyday school life misadventures of Bob as he tries to win the affection of the Class President.

Here's the list of chapters released in the 300 Perfect Game series.

  • Chapter 1 - Mga Basag na Banat ni Bob
  • Chapter 2 - Death and Rebirth
  • Chapter 3 - Samurai Z
  • Chapter 4 - Samurai BallZ
  • Chapter 5 - The Girl Who Killed Valentine's Day
  • Chapter 6 - Silvertoes
  • Chapter 7 - Slice of Life
  • Chapter 8 - Oplan Akyat Ligaw
  • Special - Seasonal Random Drama Scenes
